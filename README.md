# Beauty Brand Campaign Analysis

## Objective
This project analyzes how beauty brands use digital marketing campaigns and social media strategies to engage Gen Z consumers.

## Brands Analyzed
- NIVEA
- Rare Beauty
- Fenty Beauty
- L'Oréal

## Focus Areas
- Influencer marketing
- Campaign messaging
- Consumer targeting
- Digital brand communication
- Gen Z marketing trends

## Research Sources
- TikTok official brand accounts
- Instagram campaigns
- YouTube marketing videos
- Product launch campaigns

## Key Insights
- Authentic and relatable content performs well with Gen Z audiences
- Influencer collaborations improve engagement and trust
- Short-form video content increases interaction rates
- Emotional storytelling strengthens brand identity
