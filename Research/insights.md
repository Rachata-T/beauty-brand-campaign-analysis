# Marketing Insights

## Common Gen Z Marketing Trends

- Authentic and relatable messaging performs better than traditional advertising
- Influencer collaborations increase trust and engagement
- Short-form video content drives high interaction
- Emotional storytelling strengthens brand loyalty
- Inclusive branding improves audience connection
