# L'Oréal Campaign

## Observations

- Combines educational and aspirational marketing
- Frequently highlights skincare science and product effectiveness
- Uses influencer partnerships to increase product credibility
- Focuses on modern beauty trends and digital engagement

## Marketing Channels

- Instagram
- TikTok
- YouTube Shorts

## Key Insight

L'Oréal balances professional brand image with trend-driven digital content to attract younger consumers.
