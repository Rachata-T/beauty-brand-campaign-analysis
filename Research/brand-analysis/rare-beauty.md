# Rare Beauty Campaign Analysis

## Campaign Focus
Rare Beauty focuses on authenticity, emotional connection, and self-confidence through social-first marketing campaigns.

## Observations
- Uses relatable storytelling and mental health messaging
- Collaborates with lifestyle and beauty influencers
- Promotes natural beauty and inclusivity
- Strong engagement through TikTok and Instagram content

## Marketing Channels
- TikTok
- Instagram
- YouTube

## Key Insight
Rare Beauty succeeds by creating emotionally relatable content that aligns with Gen Z values and identity.
