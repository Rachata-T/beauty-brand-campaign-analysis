# Fenty Beauty Campaign

## Observations

- Promotes diversity and inclusivity in beauty
- Uses bold and confident visual branding
- Frequently collaborates with celebrities and influencers
- Strong focus on self-expression and individuality

## Marketing Channels

- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube

## Key Insight

Fenty Beauty successfully connects with Gen Z audiences through inclusive branding and culturally relevant content.

## Audience Target

- Gen Z consumers
- Beauty and skincare audiences
- Social media users interested in self-expression

## Campaign Style

- Bold visuals
- Inclusive messaging
- Influencer-focused promotion

## Example Campaigns

- Fenty Beauty TikTok Campaigns
- Product launch collaborations
- Influencer beauty tutorials

## Key Takeaways

- Strong emotional branding improves Gen Z engagement
- Influencer collaborations increase campaign visibility
- Short-form video content performs well for beauty brands
