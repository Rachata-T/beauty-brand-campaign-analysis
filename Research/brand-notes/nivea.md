# NIVEA Campaign Analysis

## Campaign Focus
NIVEA focuses on emotional storytelling, skincare education, and trust-based branding to connect with Gen Z audiences.

## Observations
- Uses influencer collaborations to build authenticity
- Focuses on skincare routines and daily self-care
- Uses soft and clean visual branding
- Frequently promotes inclusivity and emotional connection

## Marketing Channels
- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts

## Key Insight
NIVEA combines educational content with emotionally relatable messaging to increase Gen Z engagement.
